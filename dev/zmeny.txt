Yes, it is possible. Look at PluginManager class. Mainly registerPlugins(), activatePlugin() and deactivatePlugin() methods. To unregister plug-in, use PluginRegistry.unregister() method. If you want to "update" or "reload" a plug-in you need to unregister it first and than register again.
